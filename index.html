About

I am a full stack web and blockchain developer, currently working on apps in the decentralized asset management space. I previously worked in IT networking and security, where I architected and maintained diverse environments for a variety of clients. Over the past few years, I have been studying web and blockchain tech, recently graduating the Thinkful Full Stack Engineering bootcamp.
